Hon. Chief Lukman Kayode Mudashiru

Federal Legislator • Real Estate Developer • Visionary Leader

Member, House of Representatives – Ayedire/Iwo/Ola-Oluwa Federal Constituency

Personal Background

Chief Lukman Kayode Mudashiru, born July 28, 1969, hails from the illustrious Mudashiru family of Pongbala Compound, Molete Quarters, Iwo, Osun State. He is a distinguished Nigerian politician, real estate developer, and respected community leader committed to excellence, integrity, and service to humanity.

His leadership blends professionalism, discipline, and compassion — qualities that have defined both his public service and private enterprise.
